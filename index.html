About the Author

NutriTrack is the brainchild of Najma Gill, a passionate Entrepreneur, background in software engineer, health enthusiast dedicated to making healthy eating accessible and enjoyable.

With a deep understanding of computer science and with wisdom from expert on specified dietary science, user experience design, I set out to create a digital platform that empowers users to regain and sustain their energy so that they can use their full potential.
